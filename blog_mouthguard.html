Step 1 / Making Pallette

Mouthguard

You will need:

  • 1 Sisu Aeroguard Mouthguard

To begin designing Pallette, you'll need to obtain the form of your mouth. We'll use the mouthguard to both fit your teeth and to capture their form. We specifically use Sisu, because the holes will enable us to mount the sensor ribbon later (Step 7).

Start by breaking out the mouthguard.

Heat up a bowl of water from the tap, and stick the guard in to soften it. Once soft, press it up against your top teeth, using your fingers to make sure that it tightenly conforms.

With this mouthguard, we can now make an mold of your teeth using clay, which we'll use to form the Pallette device.
