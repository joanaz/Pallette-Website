Intro / Making Pallette

Instructions

Let's make Pallette!

This guide will enable you to create the Pallette Tongue Controller using our design files. We explain each step, so that developing Pallette is chill.

Pallette has three parts:

  1. An embedded sensor band

  2. A water-tight enclosure

  3. Firmware and software

The sensor band reads and understands your tongue movements using infrared and software, and the device fits in your mouth via a mouthguard and enclosure.

With this guide, you will mold the physical form to your mouth, embed and seal the sensor circuit within the form, and program the device to complete the Pallette device. Each section begins with a list of materials you need, and outlines the steps required to complete assembly.

Approximate Cost: < $100

Time Required: 1-3 weeks
