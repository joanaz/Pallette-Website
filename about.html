What is Pallette

Pallette provides greater independence to the mobility impaired.

Pallette, is a smart retainer that fits discreetly in the mouth. It works like a universal remote, and lets mobility impaired independently perform the activities that matter the most. All with the use of his tongue.

What We're Doing

We are making individual control private, discreet, and affordable.

With the surge of internet of things the landscape of devices that would be Pallette compatible are endless. The rate of decrease in the size and cost of hardware has enabled us to build the device under $100! Our team of Computer Scientists, Electrical and Mechanical engineers have been building Pallette. Our goal is to help provide greater independence to the mobility impaired. By putting the world at the tip of your tongue.
