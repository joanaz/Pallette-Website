Step 6 / Making Pallette

Seal Electronics

It's time to waterproof Pallette and complete the housing.

You will need:

  • Assembled PCB Ribbon
  • Nylon tubing
  • Silicone Suba Stopper
  • Food grade acrylated olefin heat shrink tubing
  • Soldering iron
  • Solder wire
  • Wire cutter

To begin, cut roughly 1 inch of nylon tubing such that it encompasses the pcb and switch portion of the battery module.

Once this is done, plug the battery module back into the sensor band.

Slide the battery cover tube over the battery module and cap the end using the Suba stopper.

Carefully slide the ribbon circuit with battery cover tube into the heatshrink tubing. Use the heatgun and clay imprint to shape the final enclosure. (see video)

video

Heat everything up with a heatgun until full shrinkage. While the end of the heatshrink tubing is heated (feels rubbery) and open on the micro-controller side, clamp it shut with your glove to seal it. Inspect the seal (pic).

Once everything seals, remove it from the imprint, and cap the battery tube with a stopper (pic)
