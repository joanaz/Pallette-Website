Step 7 / Making Pallette

Sewing

What you will need

The final step is to attach the enclosed Sensor Ribbon to the mouthguard. We use suture to sew them tightly together, because suture is biocompatible. You can get creative here, but it's better to sew at the narrower parts of the Sensor Ribbon, and avoid blocking the view of the IR sensors on the Sensor Board.
