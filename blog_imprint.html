Imprint

With the mouthguard from step 1, we'll make a clay imprint of your top teeth.

Break out the heat-bake modeling clay.(link to material) As in the picture below, roll it into a snake, roughly 1 inch thick.

Form an arc with this snake underneath your mouthguard, leaving roughly 1/2 inch at the ends. (pic)

Mouth imprint complete! You'll use this to align your electronics and to ensure that the final Pallette fits your mouth.
